About Me

I'm Anju, a dedicated barista originally from Japan 🇯🇵, now living in Germany 🇩🇪. I’ve spent the past few years mastering the art of coffee — from pulling the perfect espresso shot to pouring smooth, creative latte art. I believe every cup tells a story, and I love bringing people joy through small details and warm flavors. I'm always experimenting and improving.
